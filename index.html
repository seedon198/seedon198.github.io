Getting Started

To begin your hardware hacking journey, you'll need a basic setup. Don't worry; you can start with a few essential tools and equipment. Here's what we recommend:

  1. Computer: A personal computer or laptop with internet connectivity is essential for accessing our video courses and resources.
  2. Microcontroller or IoT Device: Start with a popular microcontroller board like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or ESP32. These versatile devices will be your playground for learning hardware hacking and IoT security.
  3. Soldering Iron and Tools: As you advance, soldering skills become valuable for hardware modification and repair. A basic soldering iron and a few tools will suffice to begin.
  4. Software: Familiarize yourself with programming languages like C/C++, Python, or JavaScript. These are widely used in hardware hacking projects.
