Hardware Hacking Village

Part of Seasides Cybersecurity Conference

Flipper Zero Village

Explore hands-on hacking with the Flipper Zero device, focusing on hardware hacking, RF signals, and other IoT devices. Learn and test various security scenarios using this versatile tool.

Badge Village

Learn to solder, program, and mod your conference badges, combining aesthetics with tech as you craft functional electronics.

HAM Village

Dive into amateur radio! Engage in activities around communication protocols, signal decoding, and emergency communications. Perfect for anyone interested in radio frequency tech and HAM radio licensing.

Digital Forensics

Explore real-world digital crime investigations. Learn forensic techniques to extract, analyze, and preserve digital evidence from devices and networks in simulated investigations.

WiFi Village

Engage in wireless network hacking, security analysis, and hands-on practice with WiFi protocols. Test the security of networks, learn defensive strategies, and explore tools for WiFi penetration testing.

ICS Village

Focus on Industrial Control Systems (ICS) security. Hands-on workshops cover the exploitation and defense of critical infrastructure, exploring real-world ICS vulnerabilities and security practices.

Drone Village

Get hands-on with drone hacking, including flight controls, communication interception, and GPS spoofing. Learn about drone technology, security flaws, and real-world uses of unmanned systems.

Lock Picking Village

Learn the art of physical security with lock picking! This village offers hands-on practice with different types of locks, teaching techniques to open them without keys and testing real-world physical security.

Soldering Village

Perfect your soldering skills with various electronics projects. Learn techniques for surface-mount and through-hole soldering, and get hands-on with circuit board assembly and repair.
