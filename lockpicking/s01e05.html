Lock Picking Tools

Lock picking tools are specialized instruments used to manipulate the internal components of locks to open them without the original key. These tools are essential for locksmiths, security professionals, and hobbyists to gain access to locked systems in a non-destructive manner.

  1. Tension Wrench:
    • Function: Applies rotational pressure to the lock cylinder.
    • Description: An essential tool for maintaining tension on the lock while picking. It comes in various shapes and sizes to fit different lock types.



  2. Pick:
    • Function: Manipulates the pins inside the lock.
    • Description: Picks come in various shapes, including hooks, rakes, and diamonds, each designed for different picking techniques.
      • Hook Pick: A pick with a hooked end used for single pin picking, allowing precise manipulation of individual pins.
      • Rake: A type of pick that is quickly moved in and out of the lock to "rake" the pins into position.
      • Ball Pick: A pick with a rounded tip used for wafer locks.



  3. Pick Gun:
    • Function: Rapidly strikes the pins, causing them to jump.
    • Description: A mechanical device that helps align the pins to open the lock.



  4. Bump Key:
    • Function: Causes the pins to jump and allows the lock to be turned.
    • Description: A specially cut key that, when struck, manipulates the pins inside the lock.



  5. Slim Jim:
    • Function: Unlocks car doors by manipulating the internal components.
    • Description: A flat, thin tool used to reach inside the door mechanism to unlock the car.



  6. Plug Spinner:
    • Function: Rotates the lock cylinder in the opposite direction after picking.
    • Description: Useful for locks that need to be rotated back to their original position to open.



  7. Broken Key Extractor:
    • Function: Removes broken key fragments from the lock.
    • Description: A tool with a hooked end used to extract broken pieces of a key stuck inside the lock.



  8. Wafer Lock Pick:
    • Function: Manipulates the wafers inside the lock.
    • Description: A tool specifically designed for picking wafer locks, commonly found in vehicles and some low-security applications.



  9. Disc Detainer Pick:
    • Function: Manipulates the discs inside the lock.
    • Description: A specialized tool used for picking high-security disc detainer locks.



Lock picking tools require skill and practice to use effectively. As you develop your skills, always prioritize ethics and lawful conduct to contribute positively to the field of physical security.
