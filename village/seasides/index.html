Seasides Conference 2023


This 20th, 21st to 22nd of September will be home to the Seasides InfoSec Conference 2023, giving you the chance to gain insight into the area of cyber security. We want to offer excellent courses and sessions for absolutely no cost and simulate the atmosphere of large international conferences like Blackhat/Defcon, thanks to our community-driven structure. We always strive to ensure everyone has an equal opportunity to learn.
